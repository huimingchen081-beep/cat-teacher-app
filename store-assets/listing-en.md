## Full Description

Meet Cat Teacher, your child's personal AI tutor that never sleeps. Covering elementary through high school, Cat Teacher delivers structured lessons from real Chinese textbooks, answers questions instantly, scans homework problems, and understands voice commands — all with a warm, patient feline personality.

**Key Features**

📚 **Textbook-Based Lectures (Killer Feature)**
- Built-in curriculum from official Chinese textbooks (部编版/人教版/PEP)
- Four-level lesson selection: edition → semester → unit → lesson
- Structured teaching: objectives → concepts → quiz → summary → reflection
- Cat Teacher explains like a real classroom teacher, step by step

💬 **AI Q&A Chat**
- Ask any question across 23 subjects, get instant answers
- Patient, encouraging tone — never makes students feel bad
- Session history saved for review

📷 **Photo Scan & Solve**
- Snap a photo of any problem, AI recognizes and solves it
- Supports text, math formulas, charts, and diagrams
- Shows step-by-step solutions, not just answers

🎤 **Voice Input**
- Press and hold to speak, release to send
- High-accuracy speech recognition
- Perfect for younger students and hands-free scenarios

🎯 **Subject Coverage**
- Elementary: Chinese, Math, English, Science, Moral Education
- Junior High: Chinese, Math, English, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, History, Geography, Politics
- Senior High: Chinese, Math, English, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, History, Geography, Politics

**Why Cat Teacher?**
- ✅ Available 24/7, answers whenever needed
- ✅ Patient and encouraging, never critical
- ✅ Follows real textbooks, syncs with school progress
- ✅ Photo scan saves typing effort
- ✅ Continuously updated with new features
